(M)oi!

BI developer during working hours, climber, gamer, and e-skater in the free time

I am a BI developer at Aitomation Oy where I help create Business Intelligence (BI) solutions for customers while also supporting and maintaining existing applications on Power BI and QlikView. Some of my routine tasks include a) transforming data and creating data models, b) designing and sharing reports, c) configuring report row-level security, d) migrating and integrating legacy and new report data sources, e) configuring file exports using Power BI, Power Automate, QlikView, R, Windows PowerShell and Task Scheduler.

My academic background is in Environmental Sciences, where I am currently working towards a Doctor of Science degree through the Sustainable Use of Renewable Natural Resources Doctoral Programme at the University of Helsinki. I hold a Master of Science degree from the Forest Sciences and Business Programme from the same university and a Bachelor of Engineering in Forestry from the Federal University of Espirito Santo.
